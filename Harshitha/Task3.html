About Canara Vikaas School

At Canara Vikaas, we pay special attention to the all-round development of students. Equal emphasis is laid on both classroom learning and extra-curricular activities, helping fine-tune talents and developing fitness among students, both mental and physical. Alongside sports, special emphasis is given to yoga, with students being put through a twenty-minute session of yoga and meditation each day, improving concentration and body-mind-spirit balance.
